OUR EVENTS

STAR PIANIST BALI has 4 events:

Internal Piano Competiton
This competition held once every year on the 1st quarter of the year; only members of star pianist Bali may enter. Judges are from Star Pianist Bali. Winners will be awarded a medal and certificate for their achievements

External piano competition
This competition held every 2 years on the 2nd quarter of the year,anyone can enter. Judges will be from Star pianist bali and from institutions. Winners will be awarded a medal and certificate for their achievements.

Piano workshop
This workshop held every 6 months. Guest speaker will be from star pianist bali and other instituions. Main focus is to develop fundamental skills and techniques, basic knowledge and literature about classical piano.

Piano Recital
Piano recital for any member who would like to showcase their skill and recite. Will play amongst other members and spectators in a selected building.
